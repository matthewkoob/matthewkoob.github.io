Projects

Shake Table for Double Potential Well

Designed and built a laboratory shake table and Scotch yoke drive system to convert rotary motion into controlled linear oscillation for a potential well experiment.

  • Designed and fabricated the shake table structure and drive system.
  • Integrated and aligned the drive system to achieve smooth, repeatable oscillation.

Tools: Mechanical design, manual machining, mechanical alignment and assembly



CNC-Machined Pneumatic Motor

Generated CAM toolpaths and machined components for a pneumatic air motor using CNC milling, followed by assembly and functional testing.

  • Generated CAM toolpaths for motor components using Fusion 360.
  • Set up and operated CNC mills.
  • Machined, assembled, and tested the air motor for functional operation.

Tools: CAM (Fusion 360), CNC milling, manual machining, mechanical assembly



3D-Printed Double Pendulum

Designed and fabricated a 3D-printed double pendulum system to demonstrate nonlinear dynamics and sensitivity to initial conditions.

  • Designed structural components for stability and repeatable motion.
  • Applied design-for-manufacturing principles for FDM 3D printing.
  • Iterated on geometry and tolerances to achieve smooth pendulum motion.

Tools: Mechanical design, 3D printing (FDM), prototyping, mechanical assembly



Spider Claw

Worked with a small team to design and build a hydraulic robot arm for teaching mechanical advantage, fluid power, and linkage-driven motion to grade-school students.

  • Fabricated and assembled multiple prototypes to test strength and reliability.
  • Designed and made final product within a limited budget, prioritizing simplicity and cost-effective materials.

Tools: Mechanical design, prototyping, manual fabrication, basic fluid mechanics
